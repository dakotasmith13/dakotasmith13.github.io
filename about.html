About Me

Hello, I'm Dakota, a motivated individual who recently graduated from the University of Vermont, armed with a degree in Mathematics and a minor in Computer Science. My academic journey instilled in me a deep passion for the intricacies of technology, particularly in the realms of website development and software engineering. With a keen eye for detail and a mind wired for problem-solving, I am on the lookout for remote career opportunities that challenge my skills and nurture my growth in these fields.


Currently residing in the gorgeous White Mountains of New Hampshire, I find inspiration in the natural beauty that surrounds me. Beyond the screen, I lead an active lifestyle. Tennis keeps me agile, weight lifting empowers my physicality, and the companionship of my dogs brings boundless joy to my life.


My coding journey isn't just about crafting lines of code; it's about creating seamless digital experiences, solving real-world problems, and embracing the ever-evolving landscape of technology. I believe in the power of collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning. If you're looking for a dedicated and creative enthusiast to join your team, I'm just an email away. Let's build something extraordinary together.
