About Me

Hi, I'm Dakota! I am a motivated individual who recently graduated from the University of Vermont with a degree in Mathematics and a minor in Computer Science. My academic journey instilled in me a deep passion for technology, particularly in the realms of website development and software engineering. I have a mind wired for problem-solving and am on the lookout for career opportunities that challenge my skills and nurture my growth in these fields.


Currently residing in the gorgeous White Mountains of New Hampshire, I find inspiration in the natural beauty that surrounds me. Beyond the screen, I lead an active lifestyle. I love playing tennis in the summer, weight lifting, and hanging out with my two dogs and two cats.


I believe in the power of collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning. If you're looking for a dedicated and creative enthusiast to join your team, I'm just an email away. I look forward to hearing from you!
